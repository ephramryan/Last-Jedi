Cast

Mark Hamill

Mark Hamill is best known for his portrayal of Luke Skywalker in the original Star Wars trilogy - Star Wars IV: Nová naděje (1977), Star Wars V: Impérium vrací úder (1980), and Star Wars VI: Návrat Jediů (1983) - a role he reprised in Star Wars: Síla se probouzí (2015), Star Wars: Poslední z Jediů (2017) and Star Wars: Vzestup Skywalkera (2019). He also starred and co-starred in the films Corvette Summer (1978), Velká červená jednička (1980), and Kingsman: Tajná služba (2014). Hamill's extensive voice acting work includes a long-standing role as the Joker, commencing with Batman (1992).
